Recipe #1 - Beshparmak

  • Time: 3h
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Beef 2-3kg
Onion 2-3p.
Potato 8-10p.
Dough 1kg

Step 1: In a pot put the meat, pour cold water and put on the fire.
Step 2: Cook for 3-3.5 hours at a gentle boil, covered with a lid, until the meat is soft and easily separates from the bone
Step 3: 1-1.5 hours before the end of cooking time in the broth to put 1-2 peeled onions, 1 peeled carrot, bay leaf, peppercorns and salt (you do not need to put vegetables, you can put only spices and salt).
Done!

Recipe #2 -Chorizo&mozarella gnocchi bake

  • Time: 35 mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Onion 1 finely chopped
Garlic cloves Crushed
Chorizo 120g didced

Step 1: Hear the oil in a medium pan over a medium heat. Fry onion and garlic for 8-10 mins until soft. Add the chorizo and fry for 5 mins more.
Step 2: Stir 3/4 of mozzarella and most of basil through the gnocchi. Divide mixture between six ovenproof ramekins, or put in one baking dish.
Done!
